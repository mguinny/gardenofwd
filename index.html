Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is Garden of We DO?

An agrarian community without borders, whose collective prosperity is based on producing and maintaining crops and farmland. "GWD" is the amplifier of the "Return to Nature" agenda, focused on driving food and herbal security for the community.

What is the Return to Nature Agenda?

The population of West Africa is expected to increase significantly over the next 20years. With arable lands laying fallow, Nature is the way to establish on-going security for current and future generations.

By showcasing all facets of what "We Do", we shall shine light on the lands, people and process.

Earth Care | People Care | Fair Share.

What do you really Do?

We operate various regenerative agricultural (permaculture) initiatives in Oyo State (Yorubaland) Nigeria and Eastern Province in Uganda. We are the farmers and partner with select farms to ensure adequate supply. Our products are Non GMO, All Natural, Beyond Organic.

We represent Economic Empowerment and Fair Trade Standards. A significant portion of all proceeds is allocated to land development and increasing capacity.

How can I participate?

Connect with us here, or on Social Media. There are various ways to participate, as there is alot going on and much to accomplish.

Capacity is our mission, con·ven·ience is the issue.

How are you providing Food Security?

Empowering the indigenous peoples with tools and plan. Closed loop, Regenerative systems. Supply chain solutions.

West Africa has all that it needs to be Food Sovereign. By understanding subsistence farming and its challenges, we are able to help coordinate and drive efficiencies.

How are you providing Herbal Security?

Know your farmer. Alloting significant acreages to herbal cultivation, seed preservation. Working directly with indigenous herbal farmers. Import/Export Certifications and International Shipments.

How can you prove NON GMO / All Natural Organic?

We conduct ourselves with the uttermost transparency and accountability. We transform fallow land using various practices and techniques to resort water tables and soil fertility. We are recognized as "Regenerative Agriculture Certified", by industry leading experts.

We believe that the term "Organic" and Organic Certifications have been compromised. has been Certifications via 3rd party organizations are not on our agenda at this time, cerifications coming soon.

We are advocates of Permaculture / Regenerative Agriculture, shining the light on bio-diversity and closed loop systems. Decisions we make consider the impact on nature and future generations.

Where can I find your products?

Direct via Air or Sea Cargo shipments.

We are here to connect with like minded individuals. With a notable product list, we are actively cultivating and partner with indigenous farmers that meet our standards. With full transparency we pride ourselves as purveyours of the highest quality of food/herbal ingredients from across Africa.
