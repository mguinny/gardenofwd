Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is Garden of We DO?

An agrarian community without borders, whose collective prosperity is based on producing and maintaining crops and farmland. "GWD" is the amplifier of the "Return to Nature" agenda, focused on driving youth awarness of opportunities under their feet.

Living in-tune with Nature, with expanding lands in Yorubaland (Oyo State, Nigeria), Busoga (Eastern Province, Uganda) and along the Nile River Delta in Egypt.

With the blessings of the territorial Chiefs and the support of the Local Government, we work with indigenous communities, to transform fallow lands, from a place of scarcity to a place of abundance.

What is the Return to Nature Agenda?

The population of Africa is expected to increase significantly over the next 20years. The youth are the focus and nature is the way to establish on-going security for current and future generations.

By showcasing all facets of what "We Do", we shall shine light on the lands, people and agenda.

What do you really Do?

We operate various regenerative agricultural (permaculture) initiatives, to increase Herbal & Food Security for the indigenous communities, territories, and global collective of like minded individuals.

We are the farmers and partner with select farms to ensure adequate supply. We utilize ancient wisdom and modern methodologies to establish sustainable systems. We protect the lands, seeds and eco-systems, implementing various land management and restoration techniques. With a group of international experts helping us find solutions to whatever we encounter.

We represent Economic Empowerment and Fair Trade Standards. A significant portion of all proceeds is allocated to on-going land and community development to establish beacons of agricultural prosperity.

How can I participate?

There are various ways to participate, as vast amounts of lands require proper stewardship. We operate various social media accounts, "Garden of We Do", is the brand for the collective.

How are you providing Food Security?

Empowering the indigenous peoples with tools and best practices. Implementing closed loop, regenerative systems and supply chain solutions.

The middle belt of Africa has the ability to be Food Sovereign. By understanding subsistence farming and its challenges, we are able to inspire those in need of direction. All Natural, NON GMO.

How are you providing Herbal Security?

Alloting significant acreages to "herbal" (specific plants and trees) cultivation and seed preservation under the direction and wisdom of generational knowledge.

Working directly with purveyours of various herbal ingredients, and investing in their growth.

"Babaaláwo" in Yoruba language, means "father of secrets". It is with generational knowledge and operating in-tune with nature that we are able to do what we do.

How can you prove NON GMO / All Natural Organic?

We conduct ourselves with the uttermost transparency and accountability. We transform fallow land using various practices and techniques to resort water tables and soil fertility. We are recognized as "Regenerative Agriculture Certified", by industry leading experts. We refer to what we do as "Regenerative Organic"

The term "Organic" is a new word. Everything in Africa was "Organic" 100years ago. To spend time and finanical resources to work with 3rd party organizations is not on our agenda at this time. The new word is a marketing ploy.

Decisions and actions we make consider the impact on nature and seven generations into the future, The Seventh Generation Principle.

How can I get your products?

Direct via Air or Sea Cargo shipments.

We are here to connect with like minded individuals. With a notable product list, we are working to find our way into Western Markets, direct to consumer and in bulk to distributors and wholesale.

With full transparency we pride ourselves as purveyours of the highest quality of food/herbal ingredients from across Africa.
