These Egyptian Dried Pear slices have a sweet, fruity flavour and aroma. The pears contain no additives – just 100% natural fruit and are non-GMO.

Dried Pears are a delicious low-fat, high-fibre snack. Great on hikes and road trips, or for school and work lunch boxes.

They are a convenient pantry item, particularly when fresh pears are out of season, and can be used in sweet or savoury recipes.

How to Use:

Enjoy these fabulous natural Australian dried pears as a snack on their own, perfect for lunch boxes, or combine with nuts and seeds in a trail mix. Cut into small pieces and add to muesli or granola blends.

Dried pears can be pre-soaked in a little warm water or fruit juice to soften; then added to smoothies, or pureed for sauces to top puddings, yoghurt or ice cream sundaes.

Add diced dried pear to raw food treats and muesli bars, or include it in home-baked muffins, cakes and fruit bread. Slowly stew pears for delicious desserts and compote.

Storage:

Store in an airtight container in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight. Refrigeration recommended in warm climates.

Shelf Life:

Up to 2 years when stored as above. See Best Before date.
